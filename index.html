About Orquid

Tours and Travel. Hospitality. Orquid is a travel agency specialized in arranging trips with every detail taken care of. Flights, visas, ground transportation, lodging, meals, entertainment, and 24/7 customer service availability in case you want to modify your trip or encounter any problems.

We are dedicated to crafting unforgettable journeys for our clients. With Orquid, every trip is a unique experience tailored just for you.

We will schedule and arrange your trip, yes. But we will also be available for you. We want to make sure your trip runs smoothly so your experience is unforgettable. If our customers have an awesome time, that means we succeed, that we will grow, that we will get great reviews. We love our country, and we want the world to start enjoying it as well.

We are professionals in understanding customer needs, and we want them to have the best experience ever. We want you to fall in love with the landscapes, with the food, with the people, and with the vibe.

We want our customers to feel that this is more than a vacation. THIS IS A UNIQUE EXPERIENCE. We are your point of contact for everything. We want to get to know you, and for you to get to know us. We want our customers to feel accompanied and supported at all times.

We offer the comfort and security of an all-inclusive trip but in a privileged and one-of-a-kind destination.
